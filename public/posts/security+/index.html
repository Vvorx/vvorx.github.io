How I passed Security+ in 2 weeks and how you can too

June 17, 2025

Disclaimer #

Before sitting this exam I already had a decent grasp of some of the contents the exam includes from college and university. However, I believe that if you put the time in you could achieve a similar result with no prior knowledge.

Exam Basics #

Start by watching the entire Professor Messer playlist, as it covers every single topic on the exam objectives. You should also keep track of the objectives while watching the videos.

Once you’re able to explain every exam objective at a basic level, start doing mock tests (I recommend either Dion or Professor Messer). When you begin averaging 80% on those, you’re ready to go. 🙂

(PS: You can use the free trial on Udemy to access Dion practice exams for free.)
